About

is an AI system that understands your emotional state and guides you toward better mental health. It combines emotional recognition with personalized recommendations.

🎯 How It Works

  1. Analyzes your reflection text and contextual signals
  2. Predicts your emotional state and intensity level
  3. Quantifies uncertainty to flag when unsure
  4. Recommends specific actions and optimal timing
